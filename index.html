MFA Thesis Project



Today, just 12% percent of engineers in the U.S. are women. Young women have interests, but they easily lose it due to the lack of role models and feel demoralized from gender stereotypes. How might we help them pursue engineering careers?

hello,w connects young women with industry resources and female engineers to make a change together. It brings the engineering related AR games to teach programming principles. Young women can have video chat experiences with mentors to learn, get inspired, and better define their paths beyond gender biases.
